Issues and Campaigns

Green Seniors aim to discuss and campaign issues relating to seniors, using technology for international communication and co-ordination Online campaign systems including the Govenment on line system will be used. We also aim to lobby the Government at the Houses of Parliament where appropriate Campaigns will include latest issues in the news and on-going issues such as those below:


  • Lack of investment into resarch for the prevention of dementia, altzheimers and other related diseases.
  • Problems caused to seniors because of austerity cuts eg Chiropody service cuts,increases in fuel costs.
  • Seniors in hospital - lack of Care, blockage of beds lack of home places, Leasure and education provision
  • We will highlight important national issues via the media and other organisations.
  • Share information with other organisations.
  • We will tackle local issues via local Green Senior Groups.
